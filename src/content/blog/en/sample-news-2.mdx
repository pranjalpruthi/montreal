🌟 **A NEW BABY IS BORN: ISKM's NEW BOOK RELEASE**

### Why Worship Only Kṛṣṇa?
*The Ultimate Vedic Conclusion*

For the pleasure of all Vaiṣṇavas, we would like to announce the release of ISKM's very own publication: *Why Worship Only Kṛṣṇa?*

... a book that, Kṛṣṇa willing, will serve the purpose of establishing the most confidential instruction of Kṛṣṇa — to surrender to Him utterly and worship Him alone — to the masses in a concise manner, and yet with full scriptural authority, for many centuries to come!

This book was compiled and published under the merciful instructions and guidance of **His Grace Sundar Gopāl Prabhu**, a beloved disciple of His Divine Grace A. C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupāda.

### Key Points
His Grace Sundar Gopāl Prabhu wanted a book that is a compilation of all relevant scriptural references from our Vedic literature to convince anyone that:

1.  **Kṛṣṇa is supreme**, not any demigods, or false incarnations, or impersonalism;
2.  Therefore, **only Kṛṣṇa needs to be worshiped**, and no one else;
3.  No process other than **Kṛṣṇa-bhakti**, especially chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, is effective, and needs to be adopted, in Kali-yuga — not some hodge-podge, concocted process.

This book aims to clarify the subject on the authority of exhaustive scriptural references, and yet be easily digestible for the average reader. It is a book that will be good for both the average Hindu reader, and also for our devotees while preaching to the Hindus.

All relevant evidence is arranged in a very logical format that is easy to refer to at any time.

We just received the first batch of this books from our printer yesterday, and we offered them to Śrīla Prabhupāda and Śrī Śrī Rādhā-Madanmohan!
