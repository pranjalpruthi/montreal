Hare Kṛṣṇa dear devotees. Please accept my humble obeisances. All Glories to Śrīla Prabhupāda!

We are happy to announce the launch of **“Hare Kṛṣṇa Japa-Yajña”** — a vow to collectively chant **10 million names (1 crore names)** of Kṛṣṇa by 9 July 2023.

The devotees of Singapore have started this yajña today on the auspicious Siva Ratri with the desire to please Śrī Śrī Rādhā Madanamohana. By chanting 10 millions names, they hope to please Their Lordships so that They will kindly bestow Their benediction for acquiring a property to build Their temple.

### Participation Rules
- Anyone in Singapore can participate
- Must chant in front of the Deities in the Temple Hall
- Must chant on the beads given by the temple, if one does not already own a personal 108-beads mālā
- Temple devotees will guide the participants
- Everyone is encouraged to take up a vow of how many rounds he will chant for the yajña
- After each session, one must enter his name and number of rounds chanted in the Koṭi-Nāma Japa-Yajña register maintained by the Temple
- Total rounds chanted will be updated daily on the Temple Notice Board and on social media channels
- For devotees, daily personal sādhana rounds do not count, rounds for this yajña have to be separately counted

### Counting Method
One chant of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra is:

> Hare Kṛṣṇa Hare Kṛṣṇa
> Kṛṣṇa Kṛṣṇa Hare Hare
> Hare Rāma Hare Rāma
> Rāma Rāma Hare Hare

… which is 16 names in total.

- **1 mālā (round)** = 108 beads.
- **1 mālā** = 108 x 16 names = 1,728 names.
- Approximately, **100,000 names** is calculated to be **64 rounds**.
- So, **10,000,000 (1 crore) names** = **6,400 rounds**.
