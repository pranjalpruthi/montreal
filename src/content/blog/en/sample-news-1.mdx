Hare Krsna dear devotees, please accept my humble obeisances. All glories to Srīla Prabhupāda.

We're pleased to announce our reception of **3,000 copies** of *"The Introduction Of Bhagavad Gita As It Is"*. This is surely by the mercy of Prabhupāda and Krsna due to our endeavors to make this happen through arduous endeavors.

### Book Distribution Update

By the donations of ISKM devotees in support for our endeavors to freely distribute *"Introduction to Bhagavad Gita As It Is"*, as a team in ISKM Montréal we've distributed **126 books** at a French student institution and metro station.

Hare Krsna!
