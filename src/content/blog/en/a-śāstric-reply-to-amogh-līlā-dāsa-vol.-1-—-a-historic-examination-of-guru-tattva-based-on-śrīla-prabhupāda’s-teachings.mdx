# **What You Will Find Inside Volume 1**

The eBook covers all major śāstric and historical points, including:

## **1. Proper Etiquette in Discussing Guru-tattva**

The opening chapter demonstrates how guru-tattva must be approached with gravity and humility — not through jokes or light commentary. Śrīla Prabhupāda describes the guru as _“heavy with knowledge”_ and warns against frivolous treatment of sacred topics.
(See pp. 1–2.)

## **2. The Question ISKCON Often Does Not Show**

The book restores the full May 28, 1977 question:

> _“... particularly at that time when you are no longer with us. We want to know how first and second initiations will be conducted.”_

This question is essential but often not included in ISKCON presentations.
(See p. 4.)

## **3. “Ṛtvik, yes.” — Prabhupāda’s Direct Response**

Śrīla Prabhupāda clearly confirms the system of **officiating ācāryas**, the ṛtvik representatives.
The book carefully examines the _entire_ conversation — including the parts frequently misinterpreted.
(See pp. 6–12.)

## **4. The July 9, 1977 Final Directive**

The eBook includes the full text of Śrīla Prabhupāda’s final institutional order, addressed to all GBCs and temple presidents:

- Names of 11 representatives
- Clear statement that _“the newly initiated devotees are disciples of Śrīla Prabhupāda”_
- The key word: **“henceforward”**

The book analyzes why this letter stands as the **formal and conclusive system** for initiations.
(See pp. 14–20.)

## **5. Śikṣā-Guru vs. Dīkṣā-Guru**

A detailed explanation of how śikṣā-gurus provide daily guidance, while divya-jñāna — the essence of dīkṣā — continues to come from Śrīla Prabhupāda through his vāṇī.
(See pp. 21–26.)

## **6. Sentimentality vs. Śāstric Qualification**

The eBook stresses that **years of service** do not automatically qualify someone as a dīkṣā-guru. Śāstra emphasizes brahma-niṣṭhā — pure, unwavering spiritual realization.
(See pp. 27–30.)

## **7. Nyāya’s Para-Vidhi Principle**

A crucial section showing that **the later order overrides earlier general instructions**— meaning Śrīla Prabhupāda’s July 9th directive is the governing instruction.
(See pp. 38–40.)

## **8. The Absolute Need to Follow Guru’s Direct Order**

Citing CC Ādi 12.9–10, the book powerfully demonstrates:

> _“Anyone who disobeys the order of the spiritual master immediately becomes useless.”_

This principle is applied directly to the question of guru-tattva in ISKCON today.
(See pp. 48–50.)
