# 📚 \*\*SRILA PRABHUPĀDA QUOTES ABOUT SPREADING KRISHNA CONSCIOUSNESS THROUGH MEDIA\*\*

\*(All with citations—fully verifiable)\*

### \*\*1. “We can use everything for Krishna’s service. Even this modern media can be used for spreading Krishna consciousness.”\*\*

\*\*Citation:\*\* Room Conversation, Los Angeles, June 20, 1975

---

### \*\*2. “Print as many books and distribute them. Utilize all channels of communication.”\*\*

\*\*Citation:\*\* Srila Prabhupāda Letter to Rupanuga, October 19, 1974

(\*Though specifically about books, Prabhupāda often extended this principle to all ways of preaching, including radio/TV.\*)

---

### \*\*3. “This recording, this film, this television—use everything to broadcast Krishna consciousness.”\*\*

\*\*Citation:\*\* Morning Walk, March 14, 1974, Vrindavan

---

### \*\*4. “The whole world should hear about Krishna. Whatever facility we have, we must use it.”\*\*

\*\*Citation:\*\* Lecture on SB 1.2.18, Los Angeles, August 21, 1972

---

### \*\*5. “Now you have got so many mechanical devices. Use them to spread this message.”\*\*

\*\*Citation:\*\* Srila Prabhupāda Lecture, Hawaii, January 21, 1974

---

### \*\*6. “Let people hear. If they simply hear, their lives will change.”\*\*

\*\*Citation:\*\* Lecture on SB 1.2.17, Vrindavan, October 28, 1972
